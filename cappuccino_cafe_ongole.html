Our story

A cafe that
feels like yours.

Cappuccino Cafe started with one simple belief — that great coffee and warm hospitality should be accessible to everyone in Ongole. We blend tradition with craft, serving everything from frothy cappuccinos to quick bites that hit the spot.

Whether you're catching up with friends, grabbing a morning brew, or just looking for a peaceful corner to sit — our doors are always open.
